PROJECT: Branching Paths

Push forward, and conquer regret.

The legendary hero Bitz has been kidnapped by the vengeful Demon King.
It's now up to his sister Onia as she embarks on an unforgettable journey
as she sets her sights on new paths, forming bonds and fighting new enemies
standing in her way. Will she be consumed by the seeking feeling of regret,
or will she come home with her brother, safe and sound...?

PROJECT Branching Paths is an action-packed RPG roguelike where the player has
the ability to heavily influence the power of their selected characters around
the pace of their runs. With the brand new Branch mechanic, a player can pick
and choose the abilities of a character, allowing for endless replayability.
PROJECT Branching Paths aims to tell a story or regret and what it means to
accept it or to run from it.

Inspired by RPGs new and old, like Final Fantasy XIV, LIVE-A-LIVE, Bravely Default,
and Mewgenics, PROJECT Branching Paths aims to be an RPG that pays homage
to what came before while also providing an unforgettable and meaningful experience
that can be played endlessly.
