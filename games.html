PROJECT Branching Paths

Push forward and conquer regret.

The legendary hero Bitz has been kidnapped by the vengeful Demon King. It's now up to his sister Onia as she embarks on an unforgettable journey as she sets her sights on new paths, forming bonds and fighting new enemies standing in her way. Will she be consumed by the seeking feeling of regret, or will she come home with her brother, safe and sound...?

PROJECT Branching Paths is an action-packed RPG roguelike where the player has the ability to heavily influence the power of their selected characters around the pace of their runs. With the brand new Branch mechanic, players can pick and choose the abilities of a character, opening the door for endless possibilities for party customization. PROJECT Branching Paths aims to tell a story or regret and what it means to accept it or to run from it.

Inspired by RPGs new and old, like Final Fantasy XIV, LIVE-A-LIVE, Bravely Default, and Mewgenics, PROJECT Branching Paths aims to be an RPG that pays homage to what came before while also providing an unforgettable and meaningful experience that can be played endlessly. Right now, the game is still in Alpha, but you can try it for yourself here!

Kitty's Dream Course

Run, climb and claw your way to the Dreamy Milk!

On a warm day during spring, a sleeping cat dreamed of warm, delicious milk... And now it's all she can think of! Do you think you have the skill to help Kitty through these difficult, nightmarish courses?

In Kitty's Dream Course, you play as Kitty—a cute cat with an affection for not just milk, but hats too! Using her athelitic prowess and her hat, your job is to make it to the end by performing tricks to make it to the delicious Dreamy Milk.

This is easier said than done, though! Some courses provide challenges that will test your expertise, quick-time reaction, and few have threatening enemies waiting to stop your progress. Let nothing stand in the way of Kitty's milk!

Inspired by 3D platformers both old and new like Super Mario Sunshine, Super Mario Odyssey, A Hat In Time, Psychonauts and Astro Bot, this game is made for people who itch for a fun platforming challenge.

Starshine

Accept your demons—become human.

A prince of a befallen kingdom embarks on a journey to free himself of a curse in a world ensnared by night. Can he face his demons? Or will they claim him first?

In Starshine, you take control of Cyril—the prince of the befallen kingdom—and journey across the World of Darkness to find a cure for his curse; his right arm being made of glass. It's with this arm that he will embark on his greatest journey where he'll make new friends, rekindle bonds of old and fight new enemies. Will Cyril be successful in his journey, or will he be just another demon walking the earth in search of power?

Fight furious turn-based battles and use acquired Knowledge provided by successful monster negotiations to develop strategies, and take on massive Summon battles as you take control of Cyril's demon Artorious as they take on action-packed fights of unimaginable sizes as you feel every punch with metal music performed by well-known bands! Starshine is a game that will please players looking for a story-heavy game with fun RPG mechanics with large and intense fights that will have you on the edge of your seat.

Inspired by story-heavy roleplaying game franchises FINAL FANTASY, Xenoblade, and Shin Megami Tensei, Starshine is a love-letter to the RPG genre as a whole that will bring hours of fun to its players. This is Grandberry's biggest project yet!
