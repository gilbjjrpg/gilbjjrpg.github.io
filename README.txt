8. Write a short mission statement for the business.
A: Grandberry Interactive Entertainment is an independent game development studio sitting in the heart of New York. Our primary goal isn't to

that makes sure to never limit itself and to always flex its creative muscles.

to keep an open-mind as to how our players think and feel towards our many projects. Our flexible creativity that allows us to do as we please,

know that we can hear their voices loud and clear!
