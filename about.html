About Grandberry Interactive Entertainment

Grandberry Interactive Entertainment is an independent game development studio sitting in the heart of New York. Our primary goal isn't to innovate—instead of trying to push the standard set by the industry, Grandberry Interactive Entertainment's primary focus is on providing games inspired by trail-blazers of previous generations while providing special, heart-felt twists of our own—twists that can only come from a place of love for the medium. Whether you're an individual who wants to play games with deep and engaging stories, someone who wants to play something familiar to old-school role-playing games, or someone that just wants to roam around in a open sandbox—Grandberry Interactive Entertainment is a studio that makes sure to never limit itself and to always flex its creative muscles.

Everyone here at Grandberry Interactive Entertainment feels very deeply about video games just as much as its players. We're always making sure to keep an open-mind as to how our players think and feel towards our many projects. Our flexible creativity that allows us to do as we please, while beneficial, can have a few mishaps; which is why we believe that before making any further investments to our projects that our players should be part of our decision-making process too! We provide our players with free, bite-sized demos for them to get a taste of what our team(s) have been working on and to provide them with feedback. We believe that players can help accelerate the direction of our games, and want them to know that we can hear their voices loud and clear!
